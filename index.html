What IS this website?
Incursions in EVE Online are encounters in which fleets of pilots work to drive back NPC pirate invasions in across the game's universe. They represent high-end PVE content and are equivalent to 'raids' in other MMOs. This website is a tool designed to help EVE Online players locate and track the status of Incursions within the game's universe. The contents of this page will update every 15 minutes.
